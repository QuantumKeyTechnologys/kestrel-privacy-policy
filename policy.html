Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Your Privacy Matters

Kestrel is designed with privacy as a core principle. All packet capture analysis happens entirely on your device.

What We Don't Collect

  • No Data Transmission — Your PCAP files and analysis results remains on your device.
  • No Trackers Added — We did not add any trackers, analytics services, or crash reporting tools to Kestrel.
  • No Personal Information — We do not collect, store, or process any personal information. No account creation is required to use Kestrel.
  • No Third-Party Services — Kestrel operates completely offline. We have not developed any external APIs, cloud services, or third-party integrations that receive your data.

Local Processing

All features of Kestrel operate locally on your device:

  • PCAP and PCAPNG file parsing
  • Packet analysis and flow reconstruction
  • DNS query extraction and anomaly detection
  • File carving and media extraction
  • Sensitive data scanning

Security Disclaimer

While we have designed Kestrel with security in mind and process all data locally on your device, no software application can guarantee absolute security.

Important Notice

PCAP files may contain sensitive information including passwords, authentication tokens, and personal data transmitted over networks. Users are responsible for handling their capture files appropriately and in compliance with applicable laws and regulations. We recommend analyzing only captures from networks you own or have explicit permission to monitor.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of Kestrel after any modifications indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
